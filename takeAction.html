There are many ways you can help with this cause. Below are some things you can do to help.

Buy less and Better Quality

Often with lots of trends we have become part of a society that excessively buys clothes. We never buy clothes thinking it will be in our closet for years, and that is wear the problem is at. Buying quality clothes that will last longer is better than cheap clothes. It will help towards your environmental footprint.

Stay Away From These Brands

Fash Fashion brands care about profit over the consumer. They often using toxic chemicals, get rid of a lot of waste each day, have poor labor conditions, and are not ethical or sustainable. These Brands include -NIKE -VICTORIOUS SECRET -H&M -FOREVER 21 -SHEIN -TOP SHOP -URBAN OUTFITTERS -ALI EXPRESS -HOLLISTER -BRANDY MELVILLE -AMERICAN EAGLE OUTFITTERS -GAP -TARGET

Don't Throw Away Yor Clothes!

The clothes you bought are probably non-biodegradable so they'll just spend +200 years in the landfill until they are decomposed. Here are some things you can do. -Repair it: repair it if the clothes are torn or use your creative side to change your clothes into a new piece of clothing or repair -Donate your clothes: You can donate them to your siblings, neighbours, or charity. At Plato's Closet, you will get money for giving used clothes. -Put them in Textile recycling bins or Clothing Donation bins:Textile recycling bins will make new clothes with your used clothes and clothing donation bins will give your clothes to people in 3rd-world countries or recycle them. -Write a letter to your favorite Clothing brand: Writing a letter email will make them notice about your problem as a clothing consumer.
